Owner-Operated Excellence

N.B.R. Paint Company was established in the Dallas/Fort Worth area to bridge the gap between large, impersonal contractors and high-quality craftsmanship. As a dedicated two-person team, we ensure that the owners who quote your project are the same hands performing the work.

With a diverse background spanning construction cleanup, demolition, and carpentry, we specialize in the critical details of surface preparation. We know that a premium finish—whether it's interior painting or industrial epoxy flooring—is only as good as the foundation beneath it.
